Organizations accumulate repositories over time. Some are actively maintained, but many become abandoned - no commits, no issues, no activity. These stale repos clutter your catalog, confuse new team members trying to find the right project, and can even pose security risks if they contain outdated dependencies.

The [stale-repos](https://github.com/github-community-projects/stale-repos) action scans your organization and identifies repositories that haven't seen activity in a configurable period. It generates reports that help you decide which repos to archive, transfer, or revive - keeping your open source catalog organized and up to date.
