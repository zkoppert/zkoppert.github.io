If you're running an InnerSource program, one of the hardest questions you'll face is: "How do we know it's working?" The answer requires defining what InnerSource means for your organization and then measuring the right things.

This article introduces a practical framework for measuring InnerSource at scale. It covers what InnerSource percentages actually mean - from 0% (no cross-team collaboration) to 50% (half your work is shared) - and how to use the [measure-innersource](https://github.com/github-community-projects/measure-innersource) tool to generate these reports automatically across your GitHub organization.
